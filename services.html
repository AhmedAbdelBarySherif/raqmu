    civil works

    Raqmu has the capabilities of undertaking civil construction projects within a wide range of contract values. Management, engineering and construction teams work very closely for delivering ultimate completion in perfect timings.

    Our fleet of modern equipment is always ready for the execution of cost effective solutions including construction and infrastructures at ultimate quality.

    Mep & Automation

    Raqmu pursues every technique and innovation in the execution of mechanical, electrical and plumbing works with the most efficient procedures that cut down the costs without compromising the quality in any aspect and while meeting the international LEED standards.

    We provide integrated MEP and automation services with the aid of our partners with our vast expertise in project execution in many project types from residential to commercial and industrial projects implicating health, education, manufacturing and more.

    Finishing Works

    Raqmu seeks perfection as it provides interior and exterior finishing works employing the highest quality materials and resources utilised by precisely selected talented workmen.

    We have a track-record of finishing works including painting, plastering, metal works, interfaces and landscapes among others.

    Applications

    Raqmu extends its works into epoxy, urethane cement, quartz, flake floors in addition to ramps and containments with the highest execution profession and material quality to bring luxurious feel and long life to grounds.
